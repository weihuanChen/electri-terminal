### What Are Electrical Terminals?

```text
Electrical terminals are components used to connect wires and cables to electrical systems, ensuring stable and secure current transmission.
```

---

### Types of Electrical Connections

```text
Different terminal types are designed for various connection methods, including crimping, fastening, and quick-disconnect applications.
```

---

### How to Select the Right Terminal

```text
Selecting the right terminal depends on wire size, connection type, mechanical requirements, and environmental conditions.
```

---

### Applications Across Industries

```text
Electrical terminals are widely used in industrial equipment, automotive systems, control panels, and power distribution applications.
```
