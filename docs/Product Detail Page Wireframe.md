Task:
Design a professional industrial B2B product detail page template.

Project context:
This website is an industrial B2B catalog site.
The product page must prioritize specifications, technical downloads, and inquiry conversion.

Example product:
UK-2.5B terminal block

Goals:
1. Make the product page highly technical and trustworthy
2. Prioritize product model clarity, specs, downloads, and RFQ actions
3. Keep the template reusable for different product categories
4. Improve conversion for engineering and procurement users

7. Downloads section

9. Related products / alternative models / accessories

10. FAQ section

11. Inquiry form section
